Places you must visit

Mariposa Grove

I have visited Mariposa Grove in October 31, 2020.

Mariposa Grove is in Yosemiti. It has Many humonguos squoya trees and many redwoods. It had a long trail up the mountain to the spot where all of the trees grew. It was a Sunday so we had to leave early becasue of school.



Death Valley

I have visited Death Valley in April, 2019.

Death Valley is in Northern California. It is extremely dry but it is very cool. There are a few areas that are mostly made of salt on to of the water. It was a Spring break so we were able to go hiking and sightseeing.



Yosemiti

I have visited Yosemiti from October 31, 2020 to January 5th, 2021.

Yosemiti is like a huge zoo for animals but they can roam around wherever they want to. It has many squoya trees and redwoods. It is mostly like jungle that is safe for humans. We stayed in a little cabin.
